Adlib Archive Catalogue – Identity statement (tab) – Title - Format specifc advice (field)

When describing Graphic materials refer to:

• Descriptive Cataloging of Rare Materials (Graphic Materials) 2013 DCRM (G) Section 1. Title and Statement of Responsibility Area

And conform to the:

• BIBCO Standard Record RDA Metadata Application Profile (BSR RDA MAP)

Following the recommendation of:

• Rare Books and Manuscripts Section (RBMS) of the Association of College & Research Libraries

As follows:

'Create RDA records by applying the provisions for rare materials included in the RDA BIBCO Standard Record (BSR).

This instruction applies also to agencies not producing BIBCO records.

If used, the BSR for rare materials should be followed consistently throughout the entire description;

it is not to be applied only to selected areas or elements.'Source: BSC statement on DCRM and RDA

Thus:

• All areas listed within the BSR RDA MAP within RDA Core and PCC Core Elements should be considered mandatory, unless otherwise stated

• Additional instructions, included within notes, concerning Rare Materials and Graphic Materials should be also be followed

With regard to titles for graphic materials refer to relevant entries within:

• BSR RDA Metadata Application Profile - See section 'Identifying Manifestations and Items'

For example:

• RDA Title Proper 2.3.2. and its parts, including guidance at RDA Recording Devised Titles 2.3.2.11

• RDA Recording Titles 2.3.1.4.

• Follow the advice to 'generally' avoid abridging the titles of graphic works

In addition, refer to section - Note on title:

Always make a note giving the source of title

• RDA 2.17.2.3 Title source
