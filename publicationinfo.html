Adlib Archive Catalogue - Allied Materials - Publication note

Requirement: As appropriate

Use to:

To identify any publications that are about or are based on the use, study, or analysis of the unit being described.

And

Record a citation to, or information about, a publication that is about or is based on the use, study, or analysis of the materials being described.

Provide sufficient information to indicate the relationship between the publication and the unit being described.

Apply according to the guidelines in:

•ISAD (G) 3.5.4 Publication note

With reference to:

• DACS 6.4 Publication Note (Added Value)

When describing Graphic Materials refer to:

• Refer to 7B Notes area.

• Refer to 7B8.3. Subsequent publication and other uses

And

• 7B12.2. ... other references to published descriptions ...

When describing Moving Image materials (Pilot Project 2020) refer to:

• RDA 27.1 Related Manifestation

Note on the Chicago Manual of Style

When creating citations, conform to the latest edition of the Chicago Manual of Style, as far as it is practical to do so.

• The Society of American Archivist use the Chicago Manual of Style within their own publications

• The Chicago Manual of Style is used as a guide for English language punctuation and capitalisation within RDA

• The RBMS recommend the use Chicago Manual of Style when creating citations for rare materials
