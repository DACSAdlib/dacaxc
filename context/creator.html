Adlib Archive Catalogue – Context (tab) – Creator (field)

Requirement: As appropriate

For advice on identifying a creator and applying an access point, refer to:

• ISAD (G) 3.2.1 Name of creator

• ISAD (G) Section 2.4 - 'Do not repeat information at a lower level of description that has already been given at a higher level'

With reference to:

• DACS: 2.6 Name of creator(s)

For guidelines on creating authority records for creators, refer to:

• Local procedure 'Adlib - Creating name authorities'

• RDA Section 3: Recording Attributes of Agents

With reference to the minimum requirements for Archival Authority Records:

• DACS - Chapter 9 - Archival Authority Records

• Authorized form of name

• Type of entity

• Dates of existence

• Authority record identifier.

AND NOTE

• While the inclusion of dates of existence within an authority, as a minimum requirement, does not prescribe that dates should be included within the Authorised Form of Name where there is no need to qualify, or disambiguate.

• Where an authority exists in either the Library Congress Name Authority files, or within Libraries Australia Authorities and the form of name is not qualified with dates, do not include dates in the form of name if there is no requirement.

• Where an authority exists in LCNA or LAA, and there is no reference to dates of existence within the coded fields, scope notes or non-preferred names, then update these fields but retain the Authorised Form of Name.
