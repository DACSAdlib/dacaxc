Adlib Archive Catalogue – Identity statement (tab) – Title - Graphic Materials (field)

When describing Graphic materials refer to:

• Descriptive Cataloging of Rare Materials (Graphic Materials) 2013 DCRM (G) Section 1. Title and Statement of Responsibility Area

And conform to the:

• BIBCO Standard Record RDA Metadata Application Profile (BSR RDA MAP)

Thus:

• Do not apply the instruction at DCRM (G) 1A1 'Enclose the general material designation [GMD] in square bracket'

• When supplying or devising a title - Do not apply the instruction at DCRM (G) 1F to enclose titles in square brackets

• When supplying or devising a title - Ensure that a note is left, detailing that the title has been supplied or devised.
