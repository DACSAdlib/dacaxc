Adlib Archive Catalogue – Identity statement (tab) – Title (field)

Catalogue according to the principles of, and follow the general guidelines within:

• International Standard Archival Description, 2nd ed. 2011 (ISAD (G)): 3.1.2. Title

Where the above lacks specificity, and for advice on recording metadata refer to:

• Describing Archives: A Content Standard, 2013 - (DACS): 2.3 Title

Graphic materials

- For format specific advice:

• Descriptive Cataloging of Rare Materials (Graphic Materials) 2013 DCRM (G) Section 1. Title and Statement of Responsibility Area

AND conform to the:

• BIBCO Standard Record RDA Metadata Application Profile (BRS-RMAP), January 21, 2020 revision

With reference to:

• BSR -RMAP See section 'Identifying Manifestations and Items

AND

• Resource Description Access 2.3.1.4. Recording title

• RDA 2.3.2.7. Recording title proper

• RDA 2.3.2.11 Recording devised titles

• RDA 2.17.2.3 Title source

Moving Image materials

When describing Moving Image materials refer to:

• Resource Description Access 2.3.1.4. Recording title

• Resource Description Access 2.3.2.7. Recording title proper

• RDA 2.3.2.11.3 Recording devised titles for moving image resources

• RDA 2.17.2.3 Title source
