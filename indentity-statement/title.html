Adlib Archive Catalogue – Identity statement – Title

Requirement: Mandatory

Give preference to the advice and guidelines within:

• Describing Archives: A Content Standard, 2013 – (DACS) : 2.3 Title

And refer to and adhere to the general principles of:

• International Standard Archival Description, 2nd ed. 2011 (ISAD (G)): 3.1.2. Title

Titles - Cataloguing post-DACS - Things to consider:

• The inclusion of dates in titles is not mandatory

• Where the decision is made to include dates in a title, terms of estimation will no longer be abbreviated

• DACS refers only to 'formal titles' and 'devised titles' and does not make refer to 'supplied titles'.

• For more information on this - refer to the 2013 edition preface section Revision Decisions

• DACS refers to the 'Companion Standards' for advice concerning formal titles, which it states are 'most commonly found on files or items'

• Following from this DACS does not provide examples of item level devised titles. Consequently, cataloguers should refer to companion standards

• As a further consequence of the above, procedures 'Guidelines for entering free text dates and date ranges' and 'Cataloguing new manuscript collections' and 'Adlib – Guidelines for processing pictures collections' will have to be amended or withdrawn from use
