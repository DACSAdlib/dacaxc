Adlib Archive Catalogue – Identity statement (tab) – Title - Graphic Materials

Graphic materials

When describing Graphic materials follow the advice at:

• Descriptive Cataloging of Rare Materials (Graphic Materials) 2013 DCRM (G) Section 1. Title and Statement of Responsibility Area

And conform to the:

• BIBCO Standard Record RDA Metadata Application Profile (BSR RDA MAP)

Thus:

• Do not apply the instruction at DCRM (G) 1A1 to 'Enclose the general material designation [GMD] in square bracket'

• When supplying or devising a title - Do not apply the instruction at DCRM (G) 1F-1F2 to enclose titles in square brackets

• When supplying or devising a title - Ensure that a note is left, detailing that the title has been supplied or devised.

• If required, include an alternative title at Notes and description Control - Variant title field

• In general, do not apply the instruction at DCRM (G) 1B7 to abridge long titles

• The instructions at DCRM (G) 1B can be used without further reference to the BSR RDA MAP

For more information on the application of BSR RDA MAP with regard to Graphic Material titles:

• BSR RDA MAP - Graphic Materials - titles
